Maison Ullens

Belgian ready-to-wear

As part of the developer team at OmLab, I worked in collaboration with Sweden Unlimited, a creative agency based in New York. I was involved in the implementation of Maison Ullens's online shop redesign.

About

Maison Ullens was founded in 2009 by Myriam Ullens. Myriam, a world traveler, philanthropist, and entrepreneur, wanted to create clothing that made her feel confident and comfortable anywhere in the world. Like their founder, people who wear their clothes appreciate different cultures, and they welcome new experiences; they are at ease in the world..
